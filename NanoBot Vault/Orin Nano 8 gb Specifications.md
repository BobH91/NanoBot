Here's a summary of the NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano 8GB specs (current "Super" revision as of Dec 2024):

**AI Performance**

- 67 TOPS (the Super revision — a 1.7× improvement over the original) [Openzeka](https://openzeka.com/en/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/jetson-super-orin-nano-developer-kit-datasheet.pdf)

**GPU**

- NVIDIA Ampere architecture with 1024 CUDA cores and 32 tensor cores [Openzeka](https://openzeka.com/en/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/jetson-super-orin-nano-developer-kit-datasheet.pdf)

**CPU**

- 6-core Arm Cortex-A78AE v8.2 64-bit, 1.5MB L2 + 4MB L3 cache [Openzeka](https://openzeka.com/en/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/jetson-super-orin-nano-developer-kit-datasheet.pdf)

**Memory**

- 8GB 128-bit LPDDR5 at 102 GB/s bandwidth [Openzeka](https://openzeka.com/en/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/jetson-super-orin-nano-developer-kit-datasheet.pdf)

**Storage**

- Supports SD card slot and external NVMe [Openzeka](https://openzeka.com/en/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/jetson-super-orin-nano-developer-kit-datasheet.pdf)

**Video**

- Encode: 1080p30 (via 1–2 CPU cores); Decode: 1× 4K60 (H.265), 2× 4K30, 5× 1080p60, 11× 1080p30 [Openzeka](https://openzeka.com/en/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/jetson-super-orin-nano-developer-kit-datasheet.pdf)

**Power**

- 7W–25W configurable [Openzeka](https://openzeka.com/en/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/jetson-super-orin-nano-developer-kit-datasheet.pdf)

**Dev Kit Dimensions**

- 100mm × 79mm × 21mm (including feet, carrier board, module, and thermal solution) [SparkFun Electronics](https://www.sparkfun.com/nvidia-jetson-orin-nano-developer-kit.html)

**Connectivity (Dev Kit)**

- 2× MIPI CSI-2 camera connectors (2-lane and 4-lane)
- USB 3.2 Type-C, DisplayPort, Gigabit Ethernet, M.2 for NVMe/WiFi

**Software**

- Supports NVIDIA Isaac (robotics), DeepStream (vision AI), and Riva (conversational AI) frameworks

The "Super" designation came with a software update in late 2024 that unlocked the full 67 TOPS — if you have an original Orin Nano Dev Kit, you can get the Super performance by downloading the latest software. [NVIDIA](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/autonomous-machines/embedded-systems/jetson-orin/nano-super-developer-kit/)
