Here's a full breakdown of the Raspberry Pi 5 8GB specifications:

**Processor** Broadcom BCM2712, 2.4GHz quad-core 64-bit Arm Cortex-A76 CPU, with cryptography extensions, 512KB per-core L2 caches, and a 2MB shared L3 cache. [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.com/products/raspberry-pi-5/)

**GPU** VideoCore VII GPU, supporting OpenGL ES 3.1 and Vulkan 1.3. [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.com/products/raspberry-pi-5/)

**Memory** 8GB of LPDDR4X-4267 SDRAM. [SparkFun Electronics](https://www.sparkfun.com/raspberry-pi-5-8gb.html)

**Display** Dual 4Kp60 HDMI output with HDR support, and a 4Kp60 HEVC decoder. [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.com/products/raspberry-pi-5/)

**Wireless** Dual-band 802.11ac Wi-Fi and Bluetooth 5.0 / Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.com/products/raspberry-pi-5/)

**USB** 2× USB 3.0 ports supporting simultaneous 5Gbps operation, and 2× USB 2.0 ports. [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.com/products/raspberry-pi-5/)

**Networking** Gigabit Ethernet with PoE+ support (requires a separate PoE+ HAT). [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.com/products/raspberry-pi-5/)

**Storage** microSD card slot with support for high-speed SDR104 mode. Peak SD card performance is doubled compared to previous generations. [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.com/products/raspberry-pi-5/)[SparkFun Electronics](https://www.sparkfun.com/raspberry-pi-5-8gb.html)

**Expansion / I/O** 2× 4-lane MIPI camera/display transceivers, and a PCIe 2.0 x1 interface for fast peripherals (requires a separate M.2 HAT or adapter). [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.com/products/raspberry-pi-5/)

**Other Features** A dedicated onboard power button, and a built-in real-time clock (RTC) that can maintain time/date information when powered off, when a backup battery is connected. [The Pi Hut](https://thepihut.com/products/raspberry-pi-5)

**Power** 5V/5A DC power via USB-C, with Power Delivery support. [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.com/products/raspberry-pi-5/)

**Performance vs. Pi 4** The Pi 5 delivers up to 3× faster CPU performance than the Pi 4. [PiShop.us](https://www.pishop.us/product/raspberry-pi-5-8gb/)

**Cooling Note** Active cooling is recommended — the official Pi 5 Case with integrated fan is one option. [PiShop.us](https://www.pishop.us/product/raspberry-pi-5-8gb/)
